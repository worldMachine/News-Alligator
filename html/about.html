About News Alligator

News Alligator is an open source project created by K. Brady Davis and powered by NewsAPI.org.

Articles will refresh every 15 minutes.

"All" is made up of articles from selected news sources.

"Tech" is made up of articles from selected Technology-related news and web sources.

"Business" is made up of articles from selected Business-related news and web sources.

"Crime" (/Law?) is still under construction.

"Sources" is made up of all sources provided by NewsAPI and will direct you to a page with articles from only the chosen source.
